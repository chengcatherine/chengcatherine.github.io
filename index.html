about me



Hello! My name is Catherine. I am currently a support engineer at Opower, a company transforming the way utilities relate to their customers. Opower's software provides customers with valuable insights into their energy consumption, along with personalized ways to save money and energy. My role at Opower allows me to incorporate my passion for creating a sustainable future in a data driven environment coupled with behavioral sciene techniques.

With a background in civil and environmental engineering from MIT, I have always had a passion for the role that technology plays in transforming, improving, and enhancing our daily lives. This passion, combined with my interest in sustainability and environmental conservation, extends outside of the energy sector. My ultimate goal is to transform the way the human population views waste. As our population grows and we continue to face resource constraints, we must recognize the necessity of eliminating waste.

When I'm not helping to bring energy usage insights to utility customers, I spend my time enjoying the heat and humidity of bikram yoga, running, baking vegan desserts, and sorting waste.
