about me



Hello! My name is Catherine. I am currently living in the Bay Area working as a support engineer at Opower, where I am helping utility customers save energy. As a member of Opower's support team, I help provide customers with valuable insights into their energy consumption, along with personalized ways to save money and energy. My role at Opower allows me to incorporate my passion for creating a sustainable future in a data driven environment coupled with behavioral science techniques.

I have always had a passion for the role that technology plays in transforming, improving, and enhancing our daily lives. This passion, combined with my interest in sustainability and environmental conservation, also extends outside of the energy sector. Understanding and reducing the waste that our population procudes has always been an interest of mine. I hope to one day transform the way that humans think about and treat waste. Check out one of my favorite movies, Just Eat It, to learn more.

When I'm not helping to bring energy usage insights to utility customers, I spend my time enjoying the heat and humidity of bikram yoga, running, baking vegan desserts, and sorting waste.
